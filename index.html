Ganga Namami Project

The Ganga Namami Project is an initiative aimed at cleaning and rejuvenating the Ganga River. Here are some key facts:

  • Launched in 2014, the project aims to reduce pollution in the Ganga River.
  • It focuses on sewage treatment, river surface cleaning, and biodiversity conservation.
  • The project is a part of the National Mission for Clean Ganga (NMCG).
  • Community participation is encouraged to ensure the sustainability of the project.
